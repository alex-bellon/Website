Hi, my name is Alex Bellon

I'm a sophomore computer science and math major at UT Austin.

I am interested in cybersecurity and software development. I like to practice on wargame sites as well as participate in online Capture the Flag challenges (CTFs). As an Engineering Officer for UT's cybersecurity organization, Information and Systems Society (ISSS), I write CTF challenges for the bi-weekly CTFs that we host. I am also on UT's Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (CCDC) team, where I focus on network and firewall security. Starting this semester, I am a TA for CS361: Introduction to Security.

In terms of software development, I do frontend work as the Junior Webmaster Officer for UT's chapter of ACM, and as one of the Webmasters for 'The Waitlisters'. As part of the Waitlisters, I also attend lots of hackathons where I do backend work for webapps, usually working in Python and SQL.

Outside of computer science, I like to do graphic design to keep creative and take breaks from coding. I also enjoy the performing arts, and go to see every Broadway show that comes to UT at the Bass Concert Hall. To keep active, I play Quidditch for one of UT's club teams (and yes, I mean the sport from Harry Potter!) as well as play intramural soccer with Texas ACM, longboard, and rock climb.
