Organizations

What I'm involved with on campus

Hook 'Em Arts

I am a member of the Hook 'Em Arts club at UT, a student organization dedicated to promoting and enjoying performing arts. We host Loft Events for shows that come through the Bass Concert Hall, as well as volunteer at youth performances. We also spread awareness of student shows and performances on campus.

The Waitlisters

I am a member of a hackathon team called 'The Waitlisters'. Started at HackTX 2017, we have since particpated in multiple hackathons. Most recently, our project 'PlayFuse' earned a spot in the Top 10 projects at Hacklahoma. Besides Hackathons, we also particpate in CTFs and Programming Competitions. A group of us in the team also like to attend weekly pub style trivia competitions near campus.

Austin Quidditch

I play on one of UT's club Quidditch teams, and yes, I mean the sport from Harry Potter! We are a competitive team that plays against other colleges and universities in the US Quidditch League. I'm sure you have a lot of questions about how a magical sport gets translated to the real world - click the link above to learn more!

Freshman Research Initiative

I am involved in the Freshman Research Initiative at UT, which lets freshmen engage in research opportunities in their desired STEM field. I am in the 'Autonomous Intelligent Robots' stream, where our team recently completed a project that would teach a robot to count how many people are in the computer lab through image recognition. I hope to continue this research next semester as well.

ISSS

I am a member and Engineering Officer in the Information & System Security Society, a club dedicated to cybersecurity and the like. We learn about what makes systems and programs safe or vulnerable, how the most recent bugs and vulnerabilities work, and we also frequently participate in Catpure The Flag competitions.
