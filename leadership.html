Leadership and Awards

Some of my accomplishments

Leadership

Co-Founder, Cyber Security Team Leader, Marketing Leader, Waitlisters Hackathon Team

I was one of the co-founders of the "Waitlisters", a team of CS students who compete in Hackathons, Capture the Flag Competitions (CTFs), and Programming Competitions. As Marketing Leader, I design the team logo and T-Shirts, update team social media, manage the team Slack and email. As Cyber Security Team Leader, I find CTFs for the team to participate in, as well as host informal training sessions to teach other teammates how to solve CTF challenges.

Engineering Officer, Information and Systems Security Society

As an Engineering Officer in ISSS, I am part of the team who writes the challenges for Capture the Flag competitions. The problems that my team and I write are used in the CTFs that ISSS hosts every other week.
